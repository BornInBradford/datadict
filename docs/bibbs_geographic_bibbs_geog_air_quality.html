BiB Data Dictionary

BiBBS property level air quality information

Building level air quality data. Contains measures of mean and maximum exposures to PM10, PM2.5 and NOx, using Bradford Council (CBMDC) and Department for Environment (DEFRA) data. Variables cover a range from exposure radii (from 100m to 2000m) measured using both circular buffers and street networks centred on the home address.
Usage instructions: To link to BiBBS participants, use the property_id variable to link to bibbs_geog_person.
Further information: Created by Kimon Krenz, on behalf of BiB. Please reference Krenz et al.(2023) when using this data, where more detailed methodological information can be found regarding the construction of these variables.
